HOW I GOT HERE

  • June 1996 content
  • May 2010 - April 2011

    Steven T. Gee, P.C.

    I picked this law firm after getting accepted into the Summer Youth Employment Program (SYEP) and I was asked to stay on past the program end date. This law firm is a main stay in the Chinatown community and they specialize in landlord tenant disputes. My duties involved scheduling meetings, filing, making copies, and accompanying clients to court hearings.

  • April 2011 - October 2011

    Asian Americans for Equality (AAFE)

    I took an assistant bookkeeping position at AAFE, a non-profit organization that provides housing for the Asian American senior community.

  • January 2012 - May 2012

    Kumon

    I tutored students between sixth and eigth grade in math and reading comprehension. I was responsible for making sure all assigned homework and practice problems were completed accurately.

  • June 2012 - August 2012

    Leader's Summer Day Camp

    Another counselor and I were responsible for looking over a group of 16 third graders. I helped the students with morning academics which included math and reading comprehension, watched over the students at lunch, ensured a safe and enjoyable recess time, and assisted with afternoon activities which included a variety of arts and crafts and sports.

  • August 2012 - August 2014

    Gigino Trattoria and Take Away

    I was hired first as an assistant bookkeeper and assisted with payroll. I later moved into a hostess role when asked by management. I eventually became an assistant manager, expeditor, and delivery manager. My duties involved seating guests, taking take-out orders, organizing the floor plan, creating weekly schedules for the delivery men, assigning fair delivery routes, balancing the cash register at the end of every shift

  • Fall 2014

    University at Buffalo

    This marks the beginning of my hospitality career at various New York City restaurants during winter and summer breaks from college. Although I preferred to stay at the same restaurants, that didn't always work out because of limited hours for a restaurant's current front of house staff.

  • December 2014 - August 2015

    Maloney & Porcelli

    Maloney & Porcelli is a main stay steakhouse in Midtown. I juggled seating and coat check, and worked private events in the Skylight Room. I first joined in the winter of 2014, left when my spring semester started, and then came back the following summer at the request of my manager.

  • May 2015 - August 2015

    John Dory Oyster Bar

    This was my second job for the summer of 2015. I was the night time Maitre'D and a day time server. My duties involved updating and printing menus, managing the waitlist, taking orders, bussing tables, running food, and de-escalating various customer service issues.

  • May 2016 - August 2016

    Birds & Bubbles

    I was part of this restaurant's opening team as the Maitre'D. There were various issues that I helped resolve due to the early stage of the restaurant. I assisted the general manager in making sure our social media content was consistent and up to date.

  • December 2016 - August 2017

    MAMO

    MAMO is a beautiful Italian-French restaurant in SoHo with celebrity clientele. Their motto is to provide every customer with an affectionate Italian and French Provencal dining experience. Due to that, I was able to say 'yes' to almost every customer request, allowing me to provide the most exceptional service.

  • May 2017 - August 2017

    Nickel & Diner

    This was my second job in the summer of 2017, along with MAMO. I worked mostly as a breakfast host, so I came in every morning at 7am and helped the team set up for opening.

  • December 2017 - August 2018

    Cosme

    Cosme needs no introduction. They are ranked 23 on the World's 50 Best Restaurants. Working at Cosme was a really unique experience because during the winter months we had a team of 5 at the front door juggling seating, phone calls, coat check, and wait lists. I first worked during the winter and then I was asked to notify my manager when I'd be back in the summer due to great work ethic and strong communication skills.

  • December 2019 - August 2019

    SUGARFISH by Sushi Nozawa
